Trending Academic Paths

⚙️

Engineering (B.E / B.Tech)

Top Institutes: IITs, NITs, COEP, VJTI

JEE Main: National level for NITs/IIITs. Jan & April sessions.
MHT-CET: Maharashtra state level. Focus on PCM.
JEE Advanced: For IIT admission. Only for JEE Main toppers.
🏥

MBBS / BDS

Government & Private Medical Colleges across India

NEET UG: Mandatory national entrance for all MBBS/BDS seats.
AIIMS: Top Medical Institute; admission via NEET UG score.
State Counselling: Separate state quota counselling post NEET.
💊

Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

Healthcare and Pharmaceutical Research

MHT-CET (PCB): Main entrance for Maharashtra Pharmacy colleges.
NEET UG: Also applicable for some B.Pharm institutes.
GPAT: Required for M.Pharm (Post Graduation) level.
🏛️

Architecture (B.Arch)

Top Institutes: SPA Delhi, CEPT, NIT Trichy

NATA: National Aptitude Test in Architecture — COA mandatory exam.
JEE Main Paper 2: For NIT/IIIT Architecture admission.
MHT-CET: Maharashtra state quota Architecture seats.
🌾

Agriculture & Forestry

B.Sc. Hons in Agri, Horticulture, Forestry

MHT-CET: State level entrance with 7/12 farmer weightage.
ICAR AIEEA: National level exam for All India Quota seats.
⚖️

Law (BA LLB / BBA LLB)

NLUs, Government Law Colleges, Private Institutions

CLAT: Common Law Admission Test for all National Law Universities.
AILET: NLU Delhi specific entrance exam.
MH CET Law: Maharashtra state law college admission.
📊

Management (BMS / BBA / MBA)

IIMs, SPJIMR, Symbiosis, MIT-WPU

CAT: Common Admission Test for IIMs and top B-Schools.
MAH-MBA CET: Maharashtra state MBA entrance exam.
SNAP / XAT: Symbiosis & Xavier University admission tests.
