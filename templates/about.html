About Vidyarthi Mitra

India's trusted educational guidance platform — empowering students to make informed decisions since 2010.

Our Mission

VidyarthiMitra.org was founded with a single purpose: to be every Indian student's trusted guide through the complex landscape of higher education. From choosing the right stream after 10th, to cracking entrance exams, finding the best colleges, and planning a career — we aim to be with students at every step.

We serve Maharashtra and India-wide students with accurate, up-to-date information about 150+ entrance exams, 10,000+ colleges, all major universities, admission cut-offs, and career guidance.
